# 🌟 Design Philosophy

Rather than treating a portfolio as a collection of projects, this application presents a connected digital ecosystem.

Inspired by the meaning of **"Anjum" (Stars)**, every section is designed as part of a larger constellation—where skills, leadership, achievements, and experience orbit a shared purpose.

The objective is not simply to showcase technical ability, but to demonstrate thoughtful engineering, product design, and attention to detail through every interaction.
